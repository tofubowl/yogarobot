The Tech Behind the Vision

Our AI Yoga Instructor Bot is built on a robust and cutting-edge technology stack, ensuring precision, scalability, and a seamless development experience.

NVIDIA Isaac Sim

For high-fidelity robotics simulation and realistic environment testing, crucial for refining bot movements and AI training in a virtual space.

PyTorch

The foundation for our advanced AI/ML models, enabling deep learning for precise human pose estimation and behavioral analysis.

ROS 2 (Robot Operating System 2)

Provides the robust framework for communication and control, orchestrating interactions between AI, sensors, and robotic actuators.

Docker

Ensures a consistent and reproducible development environment, streamlining setup and deployment across different systems.
